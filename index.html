The Caterpillar D5N Bulldozer

The most basic yet versitile machine. Great for use in ruff terrain. This machine has a low center of gravity enabling it to being operated safely especially on side-slope grading jobs. It sits on tracks for better traction and stability compared to rubber-tires.
